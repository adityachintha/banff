Notes used cautiously in the guide:

- Moraine Lake and Lake Louise access can depend on season, shuttle reservations, transit, parking availability, weather, and exact ticket type.
- Parking, shuttles, reservations, park entry, and activity tickets may be separate.
- Kananaskis access and stopping rules may require a Conservation Pass depending on where you stop.
- Icefields Parkway services and cell reception are limited; travelers should fuel up, download maps, and carry food/water.
- Restaurant, coffee, bar, and hangout recommendations are planning shortlists. Verify current hours, reservations, seasonal openings, closures, and wait times before going.
- Community advice from Reddit is useful for planning patterns, but official sources should be used for rules, fees, closures, and shuttle details.
- Map pins in `assets/banff-rockies-map-points.geojson` and `assets/banff-rockies-map-points.kml` are approximate planning points, not final navigation instructions.
